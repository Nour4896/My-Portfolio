Who is Nour Harrak?

Thanks for stopping by! My name is Nour Harrak, I'm a front-end designer from Mississauga, Ontario, with additional experience in Graphic and UX Design. I'm currently studying at Humber College as I begin to build the foundation for my career in Computer Programming. Beyond my technical experience, I pride myself on my strong connective people skills gained through my years working in retail, as well as my adaptability and constant drive to learn.

Outside of work, I'm an avid fan of both sports and video games! For the former, I follow all major Pittsburgh teams (Steelers, Penguins, and Pirates), as well as the Toronto Raptors and Liverpool FC. For the latter, I keep up with a wide variety of genres, but some of my favourite video games of all time include Super Mario Galaxy, The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask, Bloodborne, Halo 2, and Persona 5! I also enjoy discovering new music, with some of my favourite genres being RnB, Indie, Midwest Emo, Rap, and Pop Punk.

I'd love the chance to get in touch with you and get an opportunity to work alongside you, whether in Front-End Development, UX Design, or Graphic Design. Please head over to my contact section and let's connect!
